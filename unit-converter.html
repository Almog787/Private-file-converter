Navigating Global Measurements

The world primarily uses two systems of measurement: the Metric System (used by most of the world) and the Imperial System (used mainly in the United States). Whether you are following a recipe, planning a road trip abroad, or importing goods, converting units accurately is essential to avoid costly mistakes.
