Standard Conversion Ratios for Flawless Accuracy

Our converter is built on universally accepted mathematical constants, ensuring every conversion is perfectly accurate and reliable. Here are the core ratios:

  • ⚖️ Weight Conversion: 1 Kilogram (KG) = 2.20462 Pounds (LBS). Convert KG to LBS or LBS to KG effortlessly.
  • 📏 Length Conversion: 1 Meter (M) = 3.28084 Feet (FT). Ideal for converting Meters to Feet or Feet to Meters.
  • 🌡️ Temperature Conversion: To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, use the formula (Celsius × 9/5) + 32. For Fahrenheit to Celsius, use (Fahrenheit - 32) × 5/9. Perfect for Celsius to Fahrenheit and Fahrenheit to Celsius.
